Match #51 Da Swedish Mob (SWE MOB) vs Black Lords of Inefficiency (BLI): 229 - 103 (SQL final)

This was the match, I think, everybody was, and has been, looking forward to. BLI had won every SQL-match they played and they had won big-time. We hadn't played BLI before so, frankly, we were quite nervous about this one. The match opened up and I think every single player on the map was more or less nervous. DM6 is a map where it exists one major keypoint i.e. the red armour - rocketlauncher. If you could control that area for a whole match you are very likely the winner of the map. So at the opening of the match both teams tried to get control there, but during the first stages of the match neither of the teams managed to get some good control. After a while we did get the red armour area secured, and by that time we had more frags than BLI, and after a while they stayed in the RL 100+ room and didn't leave, but since we had the RA and the other RL there was no problems in getting a few frags on hit and runs in their room and by the end of the game, MOB held both RL areas and were totally in control of the entire map so BLI didn't stand a chance whatsoever... a total crush.

DM3, the good ol' mobster-map. This map was an own battle...the-undisputed-King-of-the-DM3 battle. BLI secured the RL at the start of DM3 and more or less held it for the whole match, we had the red armour and the super nailgun, so it was Rockets against Nails and believe it or not, we won. Two of us held the red armour (with our nails :) and the other two was hunting lonely RL-guys to spike with some nails so they could get their hands on a RL. I think this "SQL-final" is one of the best match we've ever played. We played like a team and not like a bunch of individuals like we used to. The future is our :)
